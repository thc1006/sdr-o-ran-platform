## Submission Process

### Step 1: Manuscript Central Account Setup

1. **Create Account:** https://mc.manuscriptcentral.com/comsm-ieee
2. **Complete Profile:**
   - Full name, affiliation, ORCID ID
   - Research interests
   - Contact information

### Step 2: Prepare Final Files

1. **Main Manuscript (PDF):**
   - Convert paper.md to IEEE two-column PDF
   - Include all figures and tables
   - Ensure proper formatting

2. **Cover Letter (PDF):**
   - Addressed to Guest Editors
   - Brief summary of contributions
   - Statement of relevance to special issue
   - Confirmation of originality

3. **Figures (Separate Files):**
   - Figure 1: architecture_diagram.eps/.pdf
   - Figure 2: performance_graph.eps/.pdf
   - High resolution (>300 dpi if raster)

4. **Supplementary Materials (Optional):**
   - Source code (GitHub repository link)
   - Extended performance data
   - Video demonstration link

### Step 3: Online Submission

1. **Login to Manuscript Central:**
   https://mc.manuscriptcentral.com/comsm-ieee

2. **Select "Author Center" → "Submit New Manuscript"**

3. **Manuscript Type:** Select "**June 2025/6G from the Sky**" from dropdown

4. **Upload Files:**
   - Main manuscript PDF (required)
   - Cover letter PDF (required)
   - Figures (separate files, required)
   - Supplementary materials (optional)

5. **Enter Metadata:**
   - Title, abstract, keywords
   - Author information (name, affiliation, ORCID, email)
   - Suggested reviewers (3-5 experts in NTN/O-RAN/SDR)

6. **Review and Submit:**
   - Carefully review all information
   - Generate PDF proof
   - Submit manuscript

### Step 4: Post-Submission

1. **Confirmation Email:**
   - Manuscript ID will be assigned
   - Save for future correspondence

2. **Track Status:**
   - Login to Manuscript Central to check status
   - Typical timeline:
     - Editor assignment: 1-2 weeks
     - Review process: 6-8 weeks
     - Decision notification: February 15, 2026

3. **Respond to Reviews (if required):**
   - Revision deadline: March 15, 2026
   - Address all reviewer comments
   - Provide point-by-point response
