# Author Biography

## Hsiu-Chi Tsai

**Hsiu-Chi Tsai** is an independent researcher specializing in Software-Defined Radio (SDR), Open Radio Access Networks (O-RAN), and Non-Terrestrial Networks (NTN) for next-generation wireless systems. His research focuses on the intersection of cloud-native architectures, artificial intelligence/machine learning, and standards-compliant telecommunications infrastructure. He has extensive experience in developing open-source platforms for 5G/6G research, with particular expertise in post-quantum cryptographic security and deep reinforcement learning for intelligent RAN optimization. His work emphasizes practical, production-ready implementations that bridge the gap between academic research and industry deployment. He is a member of IEEE and the IEEE Communications Society, contributing to the advancement of global connectivity through Non-Terrestrial Networks.
